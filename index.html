WHAT IS SUPPORTED LIVING?

Simply put: It's a range of services designed to help adults with developmental disabilities remain in independent community living.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE?

Our services are available to adults 18 years or older that want to live independently. Just ask your North Bay Regional Center worker for more information.

WHAT SERVICES DO WE PROVIDE?

We offer a wide range of services! Supported living services, skills assessments and living arrangement assessments.
